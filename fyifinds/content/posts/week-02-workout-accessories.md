**The Only 5 Workout Accessories You Actually Need in 2026**

Forget the gimmicks. I've tested a lot of gear over the years, and these are the only accessories that consistently make a difference:

**1. Resistance Bands (Light/Medium/Heavy)**
$20 for a set that fits in your gym bag. Perfect for warm-ups, accessory work, or travel days when you can't get to a gym.

**2. A Good Foam Roller**
Not the cheap ones from Amazon — spend the extra $15 on a TriggerPoint or Hyperice. Your IT bands will thank you.

**3. A Weighted Jump Rope**
Box jumps get boring. A weighted jump rope builds coordination, burns calories, and fits in a carry-on.

**4. Lifting Straps**
If you're doing heavy deadlifts or pull-ups, these are worth it. Not for ego weight — for grip fatigue on real working sets.

**5. A Solid Gym Bag**
Needs: separate shoe compartment, fits your water bottle, doesn't fall apart after 6 months. That's it.

**What I Skip:**
- Glute bands (they gather dust)
- Shake weights (literally useless)
- Fancy "recovery" gadgets under $50 (you're buying placebo)
