# The 30-Day Fitness Journal Challenge (Free Printable)

The 30-Day Fitness Journal Challenge (Free Printable)

I keep a fitness journal. Every workout, every meal, every weigh-in. And it's the single biggest reason I've actually stayed consistent for 6 months.

Not because I'm disciplined. Because a journal makes it impossible to lie to yourself.

Here's what happens in 30 days:

Week 1: You're pumped. Logging everything. Feeling good.

Week 2: The novelty wears off. The journal is the only thing keeping you honest.

Week 3: You start seeing patterns. "Why do I always skip legs?" "Oh, I ate 3,000 calories on Sundays."

Week 4: You adjust. You adapt. You hit your first real goal.

The Problem:
Most people quit between week 2 and 3. They don't see immediate results so they assume it's not working.

It's working. You're just not seeing it yet.

The Fix:
I created a simple journal layout that tracks:
- Daily workouts
- Calories / macros
- Weight
- Measurements
- One thing you're grateful for (mental health matters too)

It's free, it's simple, and it works.
