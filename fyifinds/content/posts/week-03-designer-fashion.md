**How to Find Designer Brands on eBay Without Getting Scammed**

Here's the truth: you don't need to pay full retail for Coach, Ralph Lauren, or Lacoste. You just need to know how to shop smart.

**The 5 Rules I Live By:**

**1. Always Check the Tags**
Legit sellers will have original tags, receipts, or at minimum, clear photos showing the label. If it's blurry, move on.

**2. Search "With Tags" + "NWT"**
NWT = "New With Tags." You'll find items people bought and never wore. Often 40-60% off retail.

**3. Know Your Brands**
Some fast fashion brands (H&M, Zara) try to look designer. Learn the telltale signs: stitching quality, hardware weight, lining material.

**4. Use the Reverse Image Search**
If the same photo shows up on 20 different listings, it's stolen from a brand website. Real sellers take their own photos.

**5. Check Return Policies**
Only buy from sellers with at least 99% positive feedback and a clear return window.
