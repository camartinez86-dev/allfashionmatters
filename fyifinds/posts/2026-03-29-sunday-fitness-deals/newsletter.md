### 💡 Why Fitness Journals Work

Keeping a fitness journal is the single biggest reason people stay consistent for 6+ months. Not because they're disciplined — because a journal makes it impossible to lie to yourself.

Track daily workouts, calories, weight, and measurements. By week 3, you start seeing patterns like "Why do I always skip legs?" or "Oh, I ate 3,000 calories on Sundays."

**The Problem:** Most people quit between week 2 and 3 because they don't see immediate results.

**The Fix:** A simple journal layout that tracks everything in one place. Link in bio for the Amazon journal I use.
