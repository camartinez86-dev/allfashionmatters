Uniqlo C 2026: Clare Waight Keller's Vision for Accessible Luxury

Published February 21, 2026 • 4 min read

TOKYO — Uniqlo has released the Spring/Summer 2026 edition of Uniqlo: C, a collaboration line designed by British fashion designer Clare Waight Keller.

"Designer quality meets everyday wearability."

Who Is Clare Waight Keller?

Clare Waight Keller is a British fashion designer who made her mark as Creative Director at Pringle of Scotland and later at Chloé in Paris. She gained worldwide recognition for designing Meghan Markle's wedding dress in 2018.

Why This Collaboration Works

Premium quality at accessible prices

Timeless, versatile design

Democratizing luxury

The 2026 Fashion Shift

This collaboration represents a major shift in fashion - designer pieces becoming accessible to the masses. It's democratizing luxury in a way that resonates with 2026 consumers who value quality over logos.
