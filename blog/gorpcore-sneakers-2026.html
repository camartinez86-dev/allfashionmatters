Gorpcore 2.0: The Sneaker Trend Surviving 2026

Published February 21, 2026 • 4 min read

LONDON — Remember when Gorpcore was about wearing technical hiking gear to the club? That's so 2022. In 2026, the trend has evolved into something far more sophisticated.

"They're trainers that could probably survive a weekend in the wild."

Welcome to Gorpcore 2.0

The new wave of sneaker trends is here — waterproof uppers, rugged soles, hiking-style laces. It's Gorpcore 2.0, a little less flex-y and a lot more functional.

Key Features

Waterproof uppers for all-weather wear

Rugged, grippy soles for traction

Hiking-style laces

Premium materials

Why It Matters

Post-pandemic wanderlust, climate-conscious dressing, and the industry embracing functionality meets style.

"Basically, it is Gorpcore 2.0 - just a little less flex-y and a lot more functional."

The Future

As we move through 2026, expect more brands on the functional sneaker bandwagon. The days of choosing between style and substance are over.
