What Makes the Air Jordan 13 Chicago So Special?

The Air Jordan 13 holds a unique place in Michael Jordan's signature shoe legacy. Released during the 1997-98 season — MJ's final championship run with the Chicago Bulls — the AJ13 introduced one of the most recognizable silhouettes in sneaker history.

The design draws inspiration from Michael Jordan's "Black Cat" nickname, featuring:

  • The holographic eye: A revolutionary 3D-like element that catches light beautifully
  • The paw-shaped outsole: Modeled after a cat's paw, reinforcing the Black Cat theme
  • The豹 (leopard) pattern: Hidden throughout the design for sharp-eyed collectors

The "Chicago" colorway specifically celebrates Jordan's team with its bold red, white, and black palette. It's not just a shoe — it's a tribute to basketball history.

How It Compares to Previous Releases

This 2026 release marks the fourth time the Air Jordan 13 Chicago has hit stores:

  • 1998 (OG): Original debut during the Bulls' second three-peat
  • 2010: First retro release, highly sought after
  • 2017: Most recent release before today
  • 2026 (Today!): The return we've been waiting for

One notable difference in this 2026 version: the red hue appears slightly brighter compared to past iterations, offering a subtle update that longtime collectors will appreciate while maintaining the classic look.
