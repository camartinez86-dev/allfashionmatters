Chunky Sneakers Are Back: 2026's Bold New Silhouette

Published March 9, 2026 | By All Fashion Matters | In Sneakers & Trends

Remember when chunky sneakers ruled the fashion world back in 2017? Those wild Balenciaga Triple S kicks and Yeezy Boost 700s that made you look like you were walking on clouds of controversy? Well, they're back—but this time, they've gone to finishing school.

After years of playing it safe with sleek, ultra-flat minimalist sneakers, the fashion world is rediscovering its love for bold, bulky silhouettes. But 2026's chunky sneaker isn't your 2017 throwback—it's smarter, more refined, and infinitely more wearable. Welcome to the new era of oversized footwear.

The Chunkier, The Better: What's Driving This Trend?

If 2025 was about playing it safe with clean designs and ultra-flat soles, 2026 is all about loosening up again. Sneakers are getting louder, chunkier, and dare we say—a little crazier.

According to fashion forecasters, the chunky sneaker comeback represents a collective fashion exhale. After the quiet luxury craze that dominated the past few years, there's a renewed appetite for statement-making footwear that commands attention.

"Unlike the Balenciagas and Yeezys of yore, where colour combos were absolutely insane and designs bordered on parody, things won't be as in-your-face in '26. Just look at the New Balance Abzorb 2000 or Pharrell Williams' Adidas Adistar Jellyfish—expect brands to bulk up again, but with a little more restraint." — British GQ

Meet the Sneakers Defining the 2026 Chunky Movement

1. New Balance 9060: The MVP of 2026

If you're looking for the sneaker that best represents this refined chunky era, look no further than the New Balance 9060. This silhouette has exploded in popularity, with new colorways dropping regularly throughout 2026.

The 9060 takes inspiration from the boastful futuristic and visible tech craft of the Y2K era, reinterpreting familiar 99X elements with a striking twist. Key features include:

  • An extended "N" logo with a distinctive diamond-shaped outsole
  • ABZORB cushioning in the sole for all-day comfort
  • Premium pig suede leather and mesh upper
  • A thicker CR device in the heel for added support

Spring 2026 brings fresh colorways like the earthy "Cortado" ($150 USD) and the bold 9060Z in grey/green and black/red ($155 USD). The New Balance 9060 proves that chunky sneakers can be both comfortable and sophisticated.

The New Balance 9060 in iconic colorways. Photo: Unsplash (Stock photo for illustration)

2. Puma Mostro XC: The "Freaky Sole" Revolution

For those who want to take the chunky trend to its logical extreme, enter the Puma Mostro XC. Designed in collaboration with Japanese designer Fumito Ganryu, this isn't just a sneaker—it's a conversation starter.

The Mostro XC features experimental midsoles that barely look like they belong on this planet. We're talking warped foams, twisted traction, and sculpted geometry straight out of a fever dream. The upper might seem low-key at first, but look down—that's when things get really weird.

As Hypebeast reported, "Fumito Ganryu unveiled a 3D-printed Puma Mostro during Paris Fashion Week 2025, marking the brands' second collaboration. The Japanese designer transformed the Mostro XC silhouette into a dialogue between technology and craftsmanship."

Retailing at around £350, the Puma Mostro XC represents the more experimental side of the chunky trend—perfect for fashion-forward individuals who want to push boundaries.

3. Nike Shox Z: The Tech-Fueled Classic

Nike has already kicked off the chunky revolution with the new Shox Z, bringing back the iconic technology that defined early 2000s sneaker culture. The signature column-like pods in the sole deliver unmistakable visual impact while providing responsive cushioning.

This is heritage chunky—nostalgic yet completely modern in execution. The Shox Z proves that brands can honor their archives while still pushing forward.

How 2026's Chunky Sneakers Are Different From 2017

You might be thinking: "Didn't we already do this?" And yes, chunky sneakers were everywhere in 2017. But here's why 2026's iteration is worth your attention:

  1. More Refined Proportions: The soles aren't quite as exaggerated as the original chunky era. Think "bold statement" rather than "clown shoes."
  2. Better Color Coordination: Gone are the wild color combinations that felt like a design accident. 2026 chunky sneakers feature cohesive, sophisticated colorways.
  3. Superior Comfort Technology: Brands have had years to perfect cushioning systems. Today's chunky sneakers don't just look comfortable—they actually are.
  4. Versatile Styling: These sneakers actually work with more than just oversized streetwear. You can dress them up or down.

How to Style 2026's Chunky Sneakers

Here's the beauty of this trend: chunky sneakers in 2026 are surprisingly versatile. Here are some styling tips:

With Tailored Trousers

Balance the bulkiness of chunky sneakers with tailored trousers. The slim fit of the pants contrasts perfectly with the shoe's chunky silhouette, creating a modern, proportion-played look.

With Oversized Streetwear

Go full 2017 nostalgia by pairing your chunky kicks with oversized hoodies and cargo pants. This is the effortless streetwear aesthetic that never truly went away.

With Mini Skirts and Dresses

For a feminine twist, style chunky sneakers with mini skirts or flowy dresses. The contrast between the girly garment and beefy sneaker creates an effortlessly cool vibe.
