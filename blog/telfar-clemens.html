There's a revolution happening in luxury, and it's being led by Telfar Clemens. The Brooklyn-born designer didn't just create bags—he created a movement.

The Equality Logo

"It's not for you—it's for everyone." — Telfar Clemens. The Equality Logo appears on every piece, regardless of price. This isn't accident—it's intentional.

Why Telfar Is Winning

  • Resale Value: Telfar bags retain 195-228% of retail value
  • Celebrity Endorsement: Beyoncé, Rihanna, and virtually every A-lister
  • Affordable Luxury: Most bags range from $150-$400

The Future of Telfar

As we move through 2026, Telfar continues to expand with new colorways, growing accessories line, and expanding retail presence.

The Bottom Line

Telfar Clemens proved that luxury doesn't have to be exclusive. With the Equality Logo, he's created something rare in fashion: a brand that truly belongs to everyone.

Liberian-American designer Telfar Clemens redefined accessibility with flat pricing, pre-order "bag security" programs, and live-pricing experiments.
