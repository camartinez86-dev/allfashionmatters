Curating style since 2011

Colm Dillane launched Kid Super in his Brooklyn apartment in 2015 with a simple mission: make clothes that actually meant something.

The People's Runway Revolution

In partnership with Brooklyn Borough Hall, Kid Super launched The People's Runway—showcasing five emerging designers during NYFW Spring 2026.

Featured Designers

  • Kent Anthony — boxy jackets with classical references
  • Shriya Myneni — identity exploration through design
  • Rojin Jung — Korean-American fusion aesthetics

From Brooklyn to Paris

By 2024, Kid Super was showing at Paris Men's Fashion Week, presenting collections that balanced streetwear roots with elevated tailoring.

The Bottom Line

Kid Super represents a new generation of designers who believe fashion should be inclusive, accessible, and meaningful. The People's Runway is just the beginning.

Designer Legacy

KidSuper · Community-first Couture

KidSuper's People's Runway turned Borough Hall into a living moodboard—lifting five emerging designers while keeping Colm Dillane's playful tailoring in the spotlight.

"If community is the runway, experiments can actually take off."

Colm Dillane stitches graffiti, soccer scarves, and surreal portraits into couture-level pieces. His People's Runway mentorship pipeline mirrors Hypebeast's focus on community uplift while creating resale-ready limited editions.

  • 2020

    LVMH Prize People’s Choice puts KidSuper on the global map.

  • 2023

    Guest directs Louis Vuitton menswear, blending cartoons with Pont Neuf opulence.

  • 2026

    Launches People's Runway with Brooklyn Borough Hall during NYFW.
