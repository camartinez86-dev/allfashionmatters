Why Supreme Still Matters in 2026

February 20, 2026 • 5 min read

When James Jebbia founded Supreme in 1994, nobody predicted it would become one of the most influential brands in fashion history. Nearly 32 years later, Supreme isn't just surviving — it's thriving.

Owning a piece of Supreme is still seen by many as a rite of passage in the streetwear world. The red box logo isn't just a design — it's a cultural statement.
