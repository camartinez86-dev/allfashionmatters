# Social Media Growth Strategy for AllFashionMatters

**Starting Point:** Zero followers  
**Goal:** Build audience organically

---

## Platform Strategy by Priority

### 1. Instagram (Highest Priority)
**Why:** Visual platform, fashion fits perfectly, fastest growth potential

**Hashtags to Use (Mix of sizes):**
- #Streetwear (10M+ posts - competitive)
- #FashionBlogger (50M+ posts)
- #LuxuryFashion (20M+ posts)
- #SneakerHead (15M+ posts)
- #Hypebeast (8M+ posts)
- #StreetStyle (100M+ posts)
- #FashionTrends (50M+ posts)
- #MensFashion (40M+ posts)
- #KicksofTheYear (500K - good for sneaker posts)
- #Supremes (200K - good for Supreme posts)

**Low-Comp Hashtags (easier to rank):**
- #AFMfinds (our brand tag - start using!)
- #NewYorkStyle (5M)
- #UrbanFashion (10M)
- #SneakerCulture (3M)

**Tactics for Zero Followers:**
1. Post 1-2 times daily
2. Use all 30 hashtags per post
3. Engage with similar accounts (like/comment on their posts)
4. Reply to every comment immediately
5. Use Stories daily (poll, Q&A, behind-scenes)
6. Collaborate with micro-influencers (1K-10K followers)
7. Post at optimal times: 10AM-1PM, 7PM-9PM EST

---

### 2. TikTok (Second Priority)
**Why:** Algorithm favors new accounts, viral potential

**Hashtags:**
- #Streetwear (50B views)
- #FYP (foryou - use in every post)
- #SneakerTok (10B views)
- #FashionTikTok (20B)
- #LuxuryTok (5B)
- #MensStyle (2B)

**Tactics:**
1. Post 2-3 times daily
2. Trending sounds + fashion content
3. "Get ready with me" style videos
4. "POV: You found the best drop" format

---

### 3. Pinterest (Third Priority)
**Why:** Evergreen traffic, SEO benefits

**Tactics:**
1. Pin blog posts
2. Use keywords in descriptions
3. Create themed boards (Nike, Luxury, Streetwear)
4. Post 5-10 pins daily

---

### 4. X/Twitter
**Why:** News breaks here, good for drops

**Tactics:**
1. Tweet about new arrivals
2. Engage in conversations
3. Use threads for content
4. Hashtags: #Streetwear #Fashion #Sneakers

---

## Content Strategy

### Post Types (Balance This Way):
- 40% Product/drop posts
- 30% Educational (fashion tips, trend explainers)
- 20% Engagement (polls, questions, giveaways)
- 10% Behind-the-scenes

### Content Calendar:
| Day | Instagram | TikTok | Pinterest |
|-----|----------|--------|-----------|
| Mon | New drops | Trend breakdown | Week overview |
| Tue | Designer spotlight | "How to style" | Product pins |
| Wed | Fashion tips | Behind scenes | Blog pins |
| Thu | Trending topics | Trending sounds | Trend pins |
| Fri | Weekend vibes | "Weekend fit" | Style pins |
| Sat | Best content | User content | Curated |
| Sun | Reels/best of week | Prep for week | Planning |
